Internships

Try it! Experience working with SDSC experts to get a taste of future options and help you pursue your dreams.

Research Experience for High School Students (REHS)

SDSC launches an exciting volunteer internship program for high school students entitled Research Experience for High School Students (REHS). As part of this program, students will be paired with an SDSC mentor and will work as part of a research team dedicated to a particular area of computational research.

UC San Diego Mentor Assistance Program (MAP)

UC San Diego’s Mentor Assistance Program (MAP) is a campus-wide program, engaging high school students in a mentoring relationship with an expert from a vast array of disciplines, from History, Marine Physical Sciences and Nanoengineering to Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Athletics and Computer Science.
